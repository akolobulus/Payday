## 🚀 **Project Name:** Zero Broke Days

**Tagline:** *Find work. Do it. Get paid today.*

---

## 💡 **Core Idea**

A mobile + web app platform where **students and fresh graduates** can easily find **verified short-term gigs** (like errands, data entry, delivery, cleaning, design, tutoring, or remote microtasks) and **receive instant payment** after job completion — all within the same day.

Zero waiting, zero scams, **zero broke days.**

---

## 🧩 **Core Features (MVP + Beyond)**

### 🏗️ 1. **Smart Gig Matching System**

* AI-powered job recommendations based on user profile, skills, and location.
* “Quick Match” mode for urgent gigs near you.
* Filters: pay range, duration, required skills, verified employers only.

### 💵 2. **Instant Payment System**

* Integrated **e-wallet** with instant transfers via:

  * Bank APIs (Moniepoint, Flutterwave, Paystack)
  * Mobile money (MTN MoMo, Opay, PalmPay)
* Option for **Crypto or USD Stablecoin** payments for freelancers.
* Instant “Withdraw to Bank” and “Split payment” for shared gigs.

### 🛡️ 3. **Escrow & Fraud Protection**

* Payment held in **escrow** until the gig is completed.
* Both worker and employer must confirm completion.
* Dispute resolution system using AI + human mediators.
* “Verified Employer” and “Verified Worker” badges (KYC, BVN, or NIN).

### 🧠 4. **AI Gig Assistant**

* “Zee” — an in-app AI assistant that:

  * Suggests gigs based on skills or schedule.
  * Generates auto proposals or CV snippets for job applications.
  * Coaches users on interview prep or negotiation.
  * Warns users about suspicious gigs or employers.

### 🎯 5. **Zero Broke Mode**

* Emergency cash advances for trusted workers who’ve completed a certain number of gigs.
* Daily streaks = trust points = instant access to 0-interest microloans.
* Example: “Worked 5 gigs in a row? You can borrow ₦5,000 instantly.”

## 📱 **User Experience (UX)**

### 👤 **For Workers**

* Simple onboarding (Name, Skill, Location, Preferred Gig Type).
* “Find Gigs Near Me” with live map integration (Google Maps API).
* Task timer to log work hours for hourly gigs.
* One-tap payment confirmation & withdrawal.
* Earnings Dashboard: Track daily/weekly/monthly income.

### 🏢 **For Employers**

* Quick gig posting with categories (urgent, remote, on-site).
* Built-in chat and video verification system.
* Rating & review system to attract top workers.
* Bulk payments for multiple workers.
* Option to tip good workers.

## 🧾 **Extra Life-Improving Features**

### 🌍 Equal Access & Inclusion

* Accessibility mode for visually impaired or low-literacy users (voice-based UI).
* Language options: English, Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Pidgin.
* Offline mode for low connectivity users (syncs when online).
* AI translation of gig descriptions into local languages.

### 🔐 Security & Verification

* Face & ID verification for users.
* Two-factor authentication.
* Location verification for on-site gigs.
* “Report Scam” and “Panic Button” for unsafe job encounters.

### 💸 Financial Tools

* **Auto-Save Mode:** Set % of each gig payment to auto-save in a vault.
* **Mini investment options:** Grow small savings safely.
* **Budget Tracker:** Helps users plan for rent, transport, and food.

### 🧰 Skill & Growth Features

* Free courses & upskilling micro-lessons (“Learn while you earn”).
* Certificate of Completion for 50+ gigs = portfolio builder.
* Mentor system: Top-rated workers can mentor newcomers.
* “Zee Boost” – AI that helps you improve profile visibility with tips.
